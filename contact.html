Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and international career guidance.

How long does the visa sponsorship process take?

The visa sponsorship process typically takes 3-12 months, depending on the country, visa type, and your specific situation. We provide detailed timelines during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What documents do I need for international job applications?

Common documents include your passport, resume/CV, educational certificates, professional licenses, work experience letters, and language proficiency certificates. We provide a comprehensive checklist based on your target country and profession.

Do you offer services in multiple languages?

Yes, our team speaks over 15 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Hindi, Arabic, and more. We can provide consultations in your preferred language to ensure clear communication.

What is your success rate for job placements?

We maintain a 92% success rate for clients who complete our full program. This includes job placement with visa sponsorship within 6 months of completing our services. Success depends on various factors including qualifications, flexibility, and market conditions.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for our services. If you're not satisfied with our initial consultation and strategy, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your first session.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our offices directly, or using our online booking system. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations to accommodate your schedule and location.
